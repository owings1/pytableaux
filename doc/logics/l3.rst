.. _L3:

********************************************
L{L3} - Łukasiewicz 3-valued Logic
********************************************

L{L3} is a three-valued logic with values V{T}, V{F}, and V{N}. It is
similar to {@K3}, but with a different, primitive conditional 
operator that respects the Law of Conditional Identity (:s:`A $ A`).
