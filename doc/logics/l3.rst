.. _L3:

************************************
:m:`L3` - Łukasiewicz 3-valued Logic
************************************

:m:`L3` is a three-valued logic with values :m:`T`, :m:`F`, and :m:`N`. It is
similar to :ref:`K3 <K3>`, but with a different, primitive conditional 
operator that respects the Law of Conditional Identity (:s:`A $ A`).
